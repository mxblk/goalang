MANIFESTO
The idea to create NGO «Generation of Adventures» appeared naturally as a consequence of existing political, economic, social and cultural situation in Ukraine and in the world.
Information technologies created conditions for common reflection on the issues of the present, improved organizational activities and made it possible to distribute independent and original content.
This is the starting point of an expedition into a new space where everyone is a responsible unit - and together – an effective infrastructure that is implemented through the embodiment of public platform initiatives and collaborations that will exist online and offline.
And let it be a real adventure, the generators of which will be us!
ABOUT

The experience of the organization is related to eco-conscious activities, such as the example of public gatherings, which last spring with the help of Facebook created a boom and many derivative activities that contributed to the formation of environmental awareness among young people, - it was an example for other active citizens, so initiatives to clean up Kyiv's parks have doubled.

The NGO participants also visited the Vilshansk Reservoir in Transcarpathia, talked to local authorities and the community in order to avoid natural collapse and pollution in that picturesque area.

In March 2019, activists of the organization maked a photo exhibition NONSENSE, with personal photos taken on film cameras from around the world. The event was more than successful, the Port Creative Hub was visited by more than 500 people in one opening evening, an indicator of success and interest among local youth in the current achievements of the organization.

The legal address of the organization is located in Troyeshchyna, so the purpose, tasks and activities will be primarily concentrated in Desnianskyi district, where in turn there is already established communication with local authorities, housing and communal services.

We plan to continue working in this area and help improve communication between residents and public utilities.
